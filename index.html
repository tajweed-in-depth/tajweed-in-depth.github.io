Tajweed In Depth

Our platform is dedicated to sharing lessons explaining key tajweed rules. You can watch the video above to learn how to use and benefit from our platform.

All of our other projects that are supportive of/required for an all encompassing approach to offer help with memorizing the quran and learning Islamic Studies can be found right here on this platform. We hope you will find benefit, learn a thing or two, and share with others by the Will of Allah The Almighty.

Islamic Studies

Edeen Academy is our platform where we offer lessons in Aqeedah, Hadeeth, Fiqh, Seerah, Adaab & Dua. There are 48 free video lessons under each subject that are between 1-5 minutes in length. These lessons cover details regarding each subject in a simple and easy to learn/memorize format.

Qur'an

Al-Murattal is our eApp for Quran that offers a selection of reciters you can listen to. It also offers translations of ayahs using Sahih International's translation. You have the ability to repeat single ayahs and/or a group of ayats, for ease of repetition and memorization. It is a simple program for listening to and memorizing the Quran.

Click the button to visit.

Qur'an Questions

On our Qur'an Questions platform, there are various activities designed to help cement/reinforce our memorization. You can test your knowledge of the next verse in order and the surah name of the ayah mentioned. Our Complete the Surah section offers questions from within a certain juz or surah, and we are expected to complete the surah from memory. These can be used to host competitions, or just to practice.
