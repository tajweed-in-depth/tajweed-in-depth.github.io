Welcome to Tajweed In Depth

With the help and assistance of Allah the Almighty, we hope to serve the Muslim Ummah by making available on this website, detailed Tajweed rules that are found in the verses of the Noble Qur'aan.

We will do our best to provide the most correct rules by having our work checked and rechecked by Scholars of the Science of Tajweed bi-ithniLLAH (By the Will of Allah).

We ask Allah to accept this humble effort from us and to make our intentions sincere and pure for His sake alone from the beginning of it, to the middle, and until the end of it, aameen.

If you benefit from this website, please keep us in your dua'a and share it with others as well. If you find any errors, please contact us at tajweedindepth@gmail.com and let us know, so we can fix it in shaa Allah, after all, we are only human and we are prone to error.

Jazakum Allah khairan
