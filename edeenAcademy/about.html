السلام عليكم ورحمة الله وبركاته

بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم، الحمد لله والصلاة والسلام على رسول الله، أمّا بعد

eDeen Academy is a resource that we hope will be of benefit to Muslims all over the world.

It contains knowledge curated from purely authentic sources from the Qur’an and Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ). We strive to ensure that we do not use any information that may be considered weak or worse. We do our best to avoid issues that may be disputed or perhaps have difference of opinion. When situations such as difference of opinion arise, we only mention the opinion that is considered to be the most correct based on the consensus of the Scholars and Allah knows best.

The Basic courses do not have their evidences (adillah) cited or mentioned in the material, for ease of focus on the main content.

The Intermediate and Advanced courses have their evidences cited and mentioned, walhamdulillah.

The Primary sources of all our courses are as follows:

- Aqeedah = Usool Thalaathah (الأصول الثلاثة)

- Hadeeth = Sahih Al-Bukhari and Muslim. (صحيح البخاري ومسلم)

- Fiqh = Umdatul Ahkaam (عمدة الأحكام)

- Seerah = The Sealed Nectar (الرحيق المختوم)

- Aadaab and Dua’a = The Fortress of a Muslim (حصن المسلم), and Sahih Al-Bukhari and Muslim. (صحيح البخاري ومسلم)

We have included a section for The 40 Hadeeth An-Nawawi. Each Hadeeth is presented in Arabic and English, along with the audio of both on each slide. The ahadeeth are split into sets of either 10 or 11. This is because the total hadeeth actually int he book are 42. Please follow the recommended instructions on the material to gain the most benefit.

We also havea section for other General Topics. This will contain other beneficial courses or material that are complimentary to our primary courses bi ithniLLAH.

We ask Allah to accept our humble efforts and to grant us success in this life and in the hereafter, aameen.
